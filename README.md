# Far Reach

**One Planet Was Never Enough.**

Far Reach is a complete 4X hex strategy game — explore, expand, exploit, and exterminate across three planets. Built as a single HTML file. No installation. No accounts. No ads.

Play at → **[farreach.itch.io/far-reach](https://farreach.itch.io/far-reach)**

---

## Overview

Far Reach takes you from medieval warlord to interstellar commander across five technology eras. Build cities, research techs, forge alliances, and — when diplomacy fails — launch ICBMs.

- **Three planets** — Earth, Moon, Mars. Each with unique terrain.
- **Five eras** — Medieval → Renaissance → Industrial → Atomic → Interstellar.
- **Four playable factions**, each with a distinct passive ability.
- **Campaign + Skirmish** modes.
- **Single HTML file** — runs entirely in your browser, no server required.

---

## Factions

| Faction | Playstyle | Passive |
|---|---|---|
| **Apex Ventures** | Tech rush | All techs cost −20%. +2CR per research. Space units cost −2CR. |
| **The Collective** | Swarm | Units cost −1CR. Free unit spawned on every city level-up. |
| **The Greys** | Head start | Begin in the Atomic Era with advanced tech already researched. |
| **The Benevolent** | Elite | HQ starts with +10HP. Ocean tiles generate +1CR/turn. Leviathans hit 3× at max Wrath. |

## Campaign

Eight missions that teach the game one system at a time, each unlocking the next feature layer.

| # | Mission | Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | First Contact | Movement & combat |
| 2 | Growing Pains | Buildings & economy |
| 3 | Age of Invention | Technology research |
| 4 | Monuments to Power | Wonders |
| 5 | The Art of War and Peace | Diplomacy |
| 6 | Beyond the Sky | Multi-planet travel |
| 7 | Arms Race | Full tech tree |
| 8 | The Final Frontier | Everything |

## Units

23 unit types across all eras and terrain types.

**Land** — Warrior, Archer, Knight, Catapult, Rifleman, Artillery, Engineer, Settler, Tank, Jet Fighter, Psychic, Astronaut, Laser Trooper, AI Robot, Colony Vehicle, Exodus Ship

**Naval** — Galley, Ironclad, Dreadnought

**Faction Unique** — Apex Drone, Commissar, Mind Probe, Tidecaller

**Late Game** — Leviathan (ocean mega-unit), ICBM (HQ-launched, 8-hex splash)

## Tech Tree

36 technologies across five eras unlock new units, city buildings, and faction abilities.

---

## Wonders

11 buildable wonders, each unique per game. Includes Great Library, Iron Citadel, Orbital Cannon, Warp Gate, Dark Forge, and more.

---

## Diplomacy

Full Civ-style relations system — propose peace, open borders, trade deals, or declare war. AI factions have distinct personalities and will react to your actions over time.

---

## Other Features

- **Commander progression** — 50 levels, 15 titles, faction skins, map size unlocks
- **Daily challenge** — seeded map changes every day; streak counter tracked locally
- **Auto-explore** — toggle for any unit to automatically reveal fog of war
- **Fog of war** — full black-shroud system with hidden ruins and village caches
- **Random events** — 20+ events including meteor strikes, plagues, and alien invasions
- **Late-game crises** — Alien Swarm, Meteorite, Solar Storm, Pandemic
- **Screen shake, unit lerp animation, parallax starfield**
- **Dynamic ambient music** — planet-specific palettes, switches on combat
- **18 procedural SFX** built from Web Audio API primitives
- **Save / load** — full game state persisted to localStorage
- **Share score** — one tap to copy a formatted result card to clipboard

## Privacy

Far Reach uses Google Analytics (anonymous usage data only). All save data is stored locally in your browser via `localStorage` and never leaves your device. No accounts, no sign-up, no personal data collected.

Full Privacy Policy accessible in-game via the pause menu.
